Q1) What are the most important features you look for in a music player app?

I look for good audio quality, no advertisements, ease of use and a clean interface to help navigate.

Q2) Do you use features like shuffle and repeat often? Would you like to see these in a music player app?

Yes, I do use them often especially repeat once I get hooked to a song. I would like to see these in the app.

Q3) How would you like your music library to be organized?

I would like my music library to be organized by artists mostly. Plus, a recently played section is also something I would like to see in music player apps.

Q4) Do you prefer theme customization?

While I am indifferent to theme customization, I do enjoy having dark mode turned on.

Q5) Do you prefer having a home screen widget or notification bar controls for quick access to playback?

Yes, I would. It is convenient and also mitigates the need to open the app directly.

Q6) Have you used apps like Spotify, Google Play Music, or Apple Music? if so, What do you like or dislike about them?

No, I personally have not used the apps mentioned in the question. My experience is limited to Mi Music only and it comes with its notable con being advertisements after every song.

Q7) Are there any features that are missing in our competitor's applications that you would prefer in this app?

A smart music discovery feature with personalized recommendations based on listening habits, plus seamless offline playlist downloads, would be great.

Q8) Are there any features that may sway you to shift to our application?

An ad-free experience, a clean interface, and seamless offline playback would definitely make me consider switching to your app. Additionally, a smart music discovery feature with personalized recommendations would be a great bonus.
