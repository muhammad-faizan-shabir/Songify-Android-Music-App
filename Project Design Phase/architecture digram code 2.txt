    Download[Download Manager\n- Provides: IDownloadService]
    Notify[Notification Manager\n- Provides: INotificationManager]
    Theme[Theme Manager\n- Provides: IThemeSelector]
    AI[AI Recommender\n- Optional\n- Provides: IAIRecommendation]
